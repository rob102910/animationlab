Why is visualization effective?

Visualization is effective as it helps people to get a grasp of what data means. This is seen in how students were happy with a grade of 96 out of 137 and yet not happy with a 72 out of 100 which was actually a better score. How data is presented may have a large effect in how someone sees it. Another reason visualization is so effective is because vision is the most developed sense which humans have. As people have evolved to detect visual patterns, visualization is very effective in communicating information.

What are trade-offs that Cairo asks us to consider when building a visualization

The trade off which Cairo mentions the most is the trade off between a graph that looks aesthetically pleasing, and one which is more informative and clear. He also makes notes about the trade off between showing information more clearly, or highlighting one aspect as the focus and losing accuracy
